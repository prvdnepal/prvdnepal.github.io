Significant benefits to Nepal


The benefits to Nepal will be significant.


Dramatic financial savings – The costs per kilometer / passenger should be less than the total
cost of ownership of a moped. This means that riders will spend less to ride the podway than to
ride a moped. Further, riders will not need to buy motorbikes or cars; they will not need to take
them for maintenance. This will save a lot of time.


Improved safety – Last year we had 247 traffic fatalities in the Kathmandu Valley – and the
number of deaths is increasing. Injuries in traffic accidents are measured in the thousands. The
Podway will eliminate nearly all traffic accidents and injuries.

Rapid transportation – Everyone knows that traffic jams in the Kathmandu slow traffic to a
crawl. By building grade separation – providing a separate micro-rail about 8 meters off the
ground – we will speed traffic by stopping only at boarding stations. In fact, if there is no need
for a pod to stop at a boarding station, we will speed through the stops. We will never stop at
intersections. We expect to see traffic move speeds accelerate from 10 km an hour to about 40
km an hour – including tops at boarding stations.


Eliminate pollution – Kathmandu has earned the position of the most polluted city in the world.
Much of that pollution is due to motorbikes, cars, trucks, and buses. This is pollution is driving
health problems at an unprecedented rate. Our pods will be powered by hydro power. Our pods
will have a life span of 25 years or more – not the 5 years we normally see with cars.


New employment and educational opportunities – Many people would like to study at schools
or take new jobs in other parts of the city – but can’t because they can’t afford the travel costs
and time. The podway will offer opportunities that were not available before.


Revolutionary tourism opportunity – As we build the podway network across the country, we
will attract tourists around the world to visit Nepal and travel on the most modern
transportation system available.
